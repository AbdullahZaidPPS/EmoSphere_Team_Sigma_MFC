Frequently Asked Questions

A video conferencing AI friend is a computer program that can simulate a human companion in a video call. It can be used to provide emotional support and companionship to people who are feeling low, sad, or depressed.

Video conferencing AI friends can offer a number of benefits for people who are feeling low, sad, or depressed like:
1. Provide companionship and emotional support
2. Help people to feel less lonely and isolated
3. Offer a non-judgmental listening ear
4. Help people to process their emotions
5. Provide tasks and activities to help people improve their mood and well-being

To use a video conferencing AI friend for emotional support, you simply need to start a video call with it. You can then talk to it about whatever you like, and it will listen and respond in a supportive and helpful way

Video conferencing AI friends are generally safe to use, but it is important to choose one from a reputable source. You should also be careful about what information you share with your AI friend, and you should avoid sharing any personal information that you would not be comfortable sharing with a human friend.

Video conferencing AI friends cannot completely replace human friends, but they can be a helpful addition to your social network. They can provide companionship and support when you need it, and they can help you to cope with difficult times. However, it is important to maintain relationships with human friends as well, as they can offer unique benefits that AI friends cannot.
